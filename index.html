Who we are

The MSU CENTERFIRE shooting club is a Registered Student Organization dedicated to recreational and competitive shooting sports.

Our members include students from a wide range of majors who share an interest in firearms and shooting.

Our mission is to provide the MSU community with a fun, safe outlet for the use of firearms for the purpose of recreation, competition, and education.

What we do

In addition to action shooting competitions like ACTS, 3-Gun, IPSC and IDPA, MSU CENTERFIRE offers a range of recreational firearms outings. Members enjoy monthly skeet and trap shoots, and pistol caliber steel plate shoots.

Members who independently compete in other shooting competitions are actively supported by the club through coaching and resources.

Membership

Membership is open to all MSU students. Students under 18 require parental permission to handle firearms. Members are required to participate in introductory range safety courses, and are encouraged to attend expanded safety training including Range Safety Officer Certification, Defensive Pistol Training and Concealed Pistol Licensing.

Membership includes:

  • A student membership at Looking Glass River Sportsman's Club, our home range.
  • Access to outdoor, centerfire 50, 75, 100 and 200 yard ranges, a trap range, and an indoor 20 yard small bore range.
  • Get 24/7 (including Summer) access to Looking Glass at a discounted rate.
  • Access to club gear, including rifles, pistols, shotguns, ammo, optics and accessories during club events.
  • Discount rates on equipment, ammunition and accessories at partner businesses like Alpha Dog Customs.
  • Learn from some of the most experienced and qualified coaches and instructors in Mid Michigan.

Membership for the 2014-2015 Academic Year is $100, or $60 per semester.

Members are encouraged, but not required, to join the NRA.
