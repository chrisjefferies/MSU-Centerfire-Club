Preserving Firearms Heritage for a New Generation

We believe this is the best way to preserve the 2nd Amendment for future generations. If you have firearms, ammunition or financial gifts, and you're looking to provide much needed support to the shooting sports, please consider donating to the MSU CENTERFIRE club, the action shooting division of the MSU Rifle and Pistol Club.

All donations are tax deductible, and we will send a receipt for all donations. For items donated, a receipt will be written for the fair market value, which will be evaluated by the capable professionals at Alpha Dog Customs.
